Hisab Pagar - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Introduction

Hisab Pagar ("we", "our", or "the app") is a staff attendance tracking and salary management app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Storage

Hisab Pagar is an offline-first application. All data you enter into the app — including business details, staff information, attendance records, and salary data — is stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as:

  • Names or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or similar technologies

Third-Party Services

Hisab Pagar does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level security features such as screen locks and encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered as we do not store any backups on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Hisab Pagar is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app is designed for business owners and managers to track staff attendance and salaries.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
